Youth movement education

Every kid deserves to move with confidence.

LiftUp Youth is a nonprofit that visits schools, parks, and libraries to teach kids the fundamentals of movement — proper squat, hinge, lunge, and push-up technique, warm-ups, agility, and injury prevention — so they build strength and confidence that lasts long after we leave.

What LiftUp Youth is

Not a gym class. A movement foundation.

We're a volunteer-powered nonprofit built around one idea: kids who learn to move well, move confidently — and that confidence carries into sports, school, and everyday life. We bring free, hands-on movement instruction directly to where kids already are.

01

School Visits

Hands-on sessions in elementary and middle schools covering squat, push-up, hinge, and lunge technique, warm-ups, cooldowns, and agility basics.

02

Community Clinics

Free clinics at local parks, open to any family — no sign-up, no equipment, no cost.

03

Library Saturdays

"Youth Fitness Saturdays" in partnership with local libraries — movement and confidence-building for the whole family.

04

Sports Readiness

Pre-season workshops that prep kids' bodies for tryouts and practice, reducing early-season injuries.

05

Guest Speakers

Physical therapists, strength coaches, dietitians, and college athletes share real-world knowledge with kids and parents.

06

Free Resource Hub

Workout plans, stretching routines, nutrition-on-a-budget guides, and parent resources — all free, all online.

Our mission

Build strength habits before kids ever set foot on a team.

Most youth injuries and dropped confidence come from one thing: nobody ever taught the basics. LiftUp Youth exists to close that gap — teaching movement fundamentals early, for free, to every kid regardless of what teams, gyms, or equipment their family can afford.

Free, always

Every clinic, workshop, and resource we offer is free. No cost is ever a barrier to learning how to move well.

Fundamentals first

We teach the movement patterns underneath every sport — not drills for one sport in particular.

Confidence is the goal

Technique is how we get there. Confidence in their own bodies is what we're actually building.

Strong Start Program

Our flagship program walks kids through six weeks of progressive, no-equipment training — from movement basics to confidence-building agility work. Built for classrooms, clinics, or home.
