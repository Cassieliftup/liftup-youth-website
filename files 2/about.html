Why we started LiftUp Youth

Kids are asked to perform before anyone teaches them how to move.

The gap we saw

Most kids reach tryouts, gym class, or a first practice having never been taught how to squat, hinge, or brace properly — and get hurt or discouraged as a result.

The access problem

Private coaching and specialty gyms aren't in reach for every family. Movement education shouldn't depend on a household's budget.

The confidence link

We've seen firsthand how quickly a kid's confidence changes once their body starts to feel capable instead of clumsy.
