Our story

Built by student athletes who saw the gap firsthand.

As student athletes, we've seen how much access to quality strength and conditioning can shape an athlete's confidence, performance, and long-term health. We've also realized that many young athletes don't have the same opportunities. While some have access to private trainers, specialized coaches, and expensive programs, many others never learn the basics of safe strength training, injury prevention, or recovery.

We started LiftUp Youth because we believe those opportunities shouldn't depend on a family's financial resources. Every young athlete deserves to learn how to move well, train safely, and build healthy habits that last a lifetime.

Our goal is to help close that gap by making evidence-based strength and conditioning education free and accessible through community workshops, online resources, and partnerships with local organizations.

Our goals

What we're working toward.

Goal 01

Make strength & conditioning education free and accessible

Deliver evidence-based strength and conditioning education through community workshops, online resources, and partnerships with local organizations.

Goal 02

Build out the free resource hub

Publish a complete library of no-equipment workouts, stretching routines, and nutrition guides so families have somewhere to go after we leave.

Goal 03

Recruit a speaker network

Partner with physical therapists, strength coaches, dietitians, and local college athletes to guest-speak at clinics and workshops.

Goal 04

Ensure every kid builds a strong foundation

Inspire confidence and lifelong fitness so every child has the chance to build a strong foundation — both on and off the field.
