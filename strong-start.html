Flagship program

Strong Start: a free 6-week course.

Six weeks of progressive, no-equipment training that takes kids from "I don't know how to squat" to moving with real confidence — built for classrooms, clinics, or home.

Start Week 1 Bring It to Your School
Format

20–30 min sessions

Three sessions a week for six weeks. No equipment required — just floor space.

Who it's for

Ages 8–14

Designed for elementary and middle schoolers, scalable up or down for a mixed-age group.

What it builds

Fundamentals + confidence

Squat, hinge, lunge, push-up technique, warm-up/cooldown habits, and beginner speed & agility.

6-week overview

Week by week.

Week 1 is ready to use now. Weeks 2–6 are in development and will unlock here as they're finished — check back or join our list to get notified.

Ready now

Week 1 — Learn to Move

Introducing the four foundational patterns and building the warm-up/cooldown habit.

  • Day 1: Dynamic warm-up basics + bodyweight squat technique (10 min drilling, 3x8 reps)
  • Day 2: Hip-hinge technique (bodyweight good mornings) + wall push-up progression
  • Day 3: Static lunge technique + full-body warm-up/cooldown routine, start to finish
