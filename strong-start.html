Flagship program

Strong Start: a free 6-week course.

Six weeks of progressive, no-equipment training that takes kids from "I don't know how to squat" to moving with real confidence — built for classrooms, clinics, or home.

Start Week 1 Bring It to Your School
Format

20–30 min sessions

Three sessions a week for six weeks. No equipment required — just floor space.

Who it's for

Ages 8–14

Designed for elementary and middle schoolers, scalable up or down for a mixed-age group.

What it builds

Fundamentals + confidence

Squat, hinge, lunge, push-up technique, warm-up/cooldown habits, and beginner speed & agility.

Week 1 — Foundations

This week's goal: learn the foundations of safe strength training by mastering basic movement patterns, warming up correctly, and building a consistent routine.

Download Week 1 Workbook (PDF)

Dynamic warm-up (5 min) — every day

  • 20 jumping jacks
  • 10 arm circles, each direction
  • 10 walking lunges (5/leg)
  • 10 leg swings, each leg

Day 1 — Lower Body + Core (2 rounds)

  • 8 squats
  • 6 incline / knee push-ups
  • 10 glute bridges
  • 20-second plank
  • 20-second wall sit

Day 2 — Upper Body + Balance (2 rounds)

  • 8 reverse lunges per leg
  • 12 band or towel rows
  • 10 bird dogs
  • 20-second side plank per side

Day 3 — Full Body Circuit (3 rounds)

  • 10 squats
  • 8 push-ups
  • 12 glute bridges
  • 20 mountain climbers
  • 30-second plank

Cool down & stretch (5 min) — every day

  • Hamstring stretch
  • Hip flexor stretch
  • Quadriceps stretch
  • Child's pose
  • Chest stretch
  • 5 slow, deep breaths

Healthy habit of the week: Drink water throughout the day and aim for 8–10 hours of sleep each night.
